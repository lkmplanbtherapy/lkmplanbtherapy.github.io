"You Tried Plan A, Let's Try Plan B..."

Plan B... Marriage and Family Therapy was founded by Lynda Martin, LMFT in October 2014. The main office location resides in the city of San Bernardino. Plan B... Marriage and Family Therapy currently is contracted through the county of San Bernardino Department of Behavioral Health for San Bernardino county and Inland Empire Health Plan (IEHP) for both San Bernardino county and Riverside county providing behavioral health services for individuals seeking individual, couples, or family therapy.
